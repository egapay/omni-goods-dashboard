About This Project

Omni-Good Solutions is a fictional company I created in order to simulate the sales data of an actual company. The company consists of shipping out supplies to their customers all across the USA. The products range from technology, furniture, and office supplies.

Project Objective

I took on the role as Data Analyst for Omni-Good Solutions.

My manager asked me to create an exploratory dashboard answering the following questions:

  1. What was our total sales number for this year: Analyze the total sales numbers for each region, category, and month.
  2. What was our top and bottom selling products?: Evaluating the top movers can help ensure that particular item stays in stock. The bottom movers can be further assessed to determine their value.
  3. How did we perform in comparsion to last year?: Compare the year-over-year % and month-over-month % changes to understand how each region performed.
  4. Which states were we selling the most products?: Investigate which states had the most sales to help ensure on-time shipping to those customers.

Dataset

For this dashboard, I used a sample dataset of orders related to company's products being placed by customers across the USA. The data contains orders from January 2014 to January 2018, containing almost 10,000 rows. The orders included information such as order date, customer name, location, category, sales, and profit.
